Education Program

Making the world a better place one step at a time.

Providing quality education, innovative mentorship and nutring care to orphan chidren in heartful-hands since-2024

Heartful-hands Charities (HHC) has two primary education programs — the PEAK Program and the RISE Program. HHC works to transform lives and promote change by breaking cycles of poverty, creating opportunities for successful futures, and encouraging a culture of giving back. Achievements: In 2024, we supported 120 students in our two main programs — 32 students in the Peak Program and 88 in the RISE Program. We also supported 300 students through our rural outreach project in Indore

We are non-profit organization dedicated to helping those in need. Our mission is to make a positive impact on the world through various projects and initiatives.

Community Outreach

HHC has a number of outreach projects working to improve communities across INDIA. HHC’s Community Education Initiatives are aimed at raising awareness and building community networks. This includes educating school children on the risks of trafficking using targeted media, distributing educational and hygiene materials for women’s menstrual health, and helping to bring the internet to rural schools.

HHC’s Anti-Trafficking Campaign

The rate of child trafficking in Indore is shocking – UNICEF estimates each year at least 13,000 indore children are trafficked to India alone. Domestic and international trafficking are both common, and many thousands of children are forced to work in hazardous jobs or sexually exploited.

HHC has partnered with Cause Vision and SASANE to produce and distribute a Indore language comic book, entitled “Chameli Goes to School”, which raises awareness and educates students about the dangers of trafficking. HHC has printed and distributed 10,000 educational comic books to children, families and schools throughout the central regions of Indore where children are especially vulnerable.
